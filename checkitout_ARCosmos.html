Project Details

AR Cosmos

This was my final project in my Unity3D VR/AR bootcamp at Grand Circus. My partner and I utilized augmented reality to bring the book “Cosmos” by Carl Sagan to life. For the proof of concept we created six different scenes and image targets. Each scene helped the user better visualize and interact with a concept in the book. We presented this app at Demo Day.
